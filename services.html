Our services

Everything you need for a calm arrival

Here you can see our main service packages for newcomers visitors and new residents in Finland.

Arrival package

This package focuses on your first hours and days in Finland.

  • Airport greeting and pick up
  • Transport to your accommodation
  • Help with luggage and first shopping
  • SIM card setup and basic guidance

Housing and settlement package

Support for everything related to your home and daily life setup.

  • Key collection and apartment check in
  • Electricity and basic contracts
  • Furniture and household shopping and delivery
  • Moving support and coordination

Language and orientation package

Help you feel confident with language and daily routines in Finland.

  • Introduction to Finnish language learning options
  • Practice for everyday phrases and situations
  • Guidance for services transport and local tools
