Our services

Start your journey in Finland smoothly

SweetCase provides complete support for newcomers visitors and new residents during their first days in Finland.

Arrival support and essential services

From the moment you enter Finland our team ensures your first days are smooth and stress free.

  • Airport pick up with car or van
  • Friendly and safe transport to your accommodation
  • Guidance for city transport and first routes
  • SIM card purchase and activation
  • Help with first day shopping and practical tips

Housing accommodation and full settlement services

Your home is the heart of your new beginning and we help you settle quickly and comfortably.

  • Key collection from housing companies hotels and accommodation providers
  • Electricity contract setup and essential utilities
  • Apartment check and condition report when you are not present
  • Basic furniture and household items purchase and setup
  • Moving services from small loads to full relocation

Language learning and orientation for your new life

Learning the language and understanding how things work in Finland helps you start strong.

  • Support for Finnish language learning from basic to daily use
  • Guidance for education options and reliable resources
  • Help with daily tasks such as shopping and public services
  • Practical orientation for the first days of life in Finland
