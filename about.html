About us

Who we are

SweetCase is a service created in Helsinki to support newcomers travelers and people starting a new life in Finland.

Our team understands how the first days in a new country can feel confusing and stressful. We combine practical help with friendly human support so that you do not have to handle everything alone.

We focus on clear communication reliable service and calm planning so that you and your family can feel at home from the very beginning.

Our values
Care: we treat every client with respect empathy and attention to detail.
Clarity: we explain every step in simple language and keep you informed.
Trust: we handle keys homes and information with full responsibility.
Calm: we plan ahead so your first days are organized and peaceful.
